Dirt Circle Dogs no longer exist

The Dirt Circle Dogs were an indi-rock band from Denver, Colorado — featuring Daniel Eisenstat, Sondra Eby, Aaron Liechty, and Miriam Suzanne. We recorded a single album, Kingdom Come. Daniel, Sondra, and Miriam have become Teacup Gorilla.

An earthy and soulful collaboration… where everything beautiful collides with the haunted remains of the heart. - Jonathan Bitz, Denver Syntax
Eisenstat sings that there is ‘No voice on the radio,‘ and perhaps, for now, that’s true. Kingdom, though, is certainly worth a private listen at home. — Cat Carroll, Onion AV Club
